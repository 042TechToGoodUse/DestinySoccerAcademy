About Us

Our organisation is a young football acaled called DESTINY SOCCER ACADEMY (DSA) based in Enugu and established to develop grass root football in Nigeria, Established in 2016, DSA has the vision to harness young football talents who will become world-class footballers, model citizens, and world leaders. This they hope to do through innovative football development programs and activities as well as educational, health, and citizenship programs meant to empower young people and strengthen families. Our Mission

To grow local talents into World-Class professional football players, and leaders

Our Vision

Harness young football talents who will become world-class footballers, model citizens, and world leaders.

Our Objectives

  • To promote and encourage the development of the total individual by combining football with educational/academic excellence
  • To use Football as a game to take young people off the crime, drugs and other social vices
  • To give young people the opportunity to express their talents and become top professionals
  • To help talented but less privileged kids in the society actualize their dreams and potentials.
  • To provide opportunity for young talented footballers to secure a football playing career around the world
  • To improve relationship between parents, guardian and their children
  • To promote discipline on and off the pitch
  • To raise awareness on Football as a professional career
  • To improve academic performance of young talents through direct and open relationship between parents, the Academy and schools
  • To promote community service culture among young people

Our Key Businesses

We are specifically focused on raising young football talents within the following age categories;

  • Under-10
  • Under-13
  • Under-16

We recognize the efforts, time, commitment needed to groom children to the level where they can become top talents. We recruit into the Academy from the age of 8 years to 16 years. Most of the recruits in these categories are all children of primary and secondary school age.
We also operate with a view to raising stars from the streets to reduce crime and other social vices by young people. The Academy is also fully responsible for indigent children who cannot afford to pay for the services at the Academy but are very talented. We try as much as possible to work with the teachers and parents of our children to ensure that there is a fair balance between the childrenâ€™s academics and football.
Lastly and importantly is our off-football trainings, designed to strengthen our childrenâ€™s life-saving skills. Understanding that as the children grow so they will need skills to navigate life especially in dealing with issues of drugs, HIV, sexual reproductive health etc.
We have also introduced community service, which is a compulsory task for all the Academy students. We want to instil discipline and the need to give back to community.
We have built good partnership with some organisations in the United States that are committed to supporting the development of grassroots football through the provision of sport equipment for the development of young talents in Enugu. In addition, we have active link to scholarship opportunities in the United States for better education and opportunity for the young talents to blossom into great footballers.
Our board of trustees are made up of renowned professional football administrators, Religious leaders, media CEO and football coaches whose wealth of experience guides the academy to provide standard and quality training for all who are in the academy. The academy though young, but has the vision to transform how young talents are groomed.
